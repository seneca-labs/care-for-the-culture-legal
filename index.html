METHODOLOGY

how we calculated everything

Every number traces to the AIDSVu spreadsheet.

The Slider

AIDSVu national PrEP totals, real headcounts from 2012 to 2023. Dot density scales to the count.

The Community Map + Data Card

Prevalence, PrEP, and new diagnoses on one map. Coral dots for HIV, green for PrEP. The data card shows all three with the gap ratio.

The Impact Screen

Uses no AIDSVu data. White dots are people unaware of PrEP, blue dots are people aware. This is an awareness metaphor, not a data claim.

The Gap Ratio

PrEP rate per 100K divided by new diagnosis rate per 100K. Direct division of two published AIDSVu numbers. Shown inside the data card for each borough.

What We're Working With

  • AIDSVu national PrEP totals (2012–2023)
  • County-level HIV prevalence per 100K
  • County-level PrEP coverage per 100K
  • County-level new diagnoses per 100K
  • Gap ratio (PrEP ÷ new diagnoses)
  • Breakdowns by race, sex, age, transmission

What We Don't Claim

  • We do not use individual-level data
  • We do not use predictive modeling
  • We make no clinical outcome claims
  • We use no AI-generated estimates
  • We have no precision beyond county level

SIGNALS

what you take home

The photo card

Your photo, tinted by your prevention journey answer. Teal if you're on PrEP. Amber if you've heard of it. Coral if you haven't. Neutral if you opted out. The color is subtle enough that it only means something if you know what it means. Underneath: your node, your data, the map. Vertical format. Texted immediately. Shareable to IG stories. It looks like a tinted party photo, not a health campaign.

SYSTEM

how data moves

1 · CONSENT

Before anything is collected

The onboarding screen explains what we ask and why.

The "prefer not to say" option is equal in size.

You can skip contact info and still get your card.

No personal information is required to participate.

2 · COLLECTION

What we gather during the flow

We only gather what the experience needs, nothing more.

Zip code

Prevention answer

One photo

Phone and IG, both optional

3 · STORAGE

Where it lives, and not for long

Hosted on GCP or AWS, encrypted at rest and in transit.

AES-256 at rest

TLS 1.2+ in transit

Access is scoped per role

Only aggregate reports go to Gilead

4 · DELETION

Nothing persists beyond 24 hours

Photos are gone in seconds, everything else by morning.

Photos are purged the moment they're delivered.

Phone numbers are deleted after the morning text.

Zip codes and answers are deleted within 24 hours.

There are no long-term profiles or archives.

SMS: Fully TCPA and 10DLC compliant API: Feeds directly into your CRM or pipeline No PII ever leaves our infrastructure

ALTERNATIVE

Web App Only

No hardware

No shipping, no setup, no install team.

Access control

QR + passcode limits it to the event.

Same experience

Same data, same feed, same morning text.

Trade-off

No controlled photo, risk of off-site uploads.

We recommend kiosk + web app for both Freddie parties.
